List of increasingly chaotic things that could be considered soup

  • Chicken noodle soup
  • Ramen
  • Cereal
  • Pasta while it's still boiling
  • Hot chocolate
  • Salad with a lot of dressing on it
  • The ocean

The Five Stages of Grief

  1. Denial: No no, of course cereal isn't soup, that's just silly.
  2. Anger: What? Now you're being ridiculous. You can't even eat it, it's too hot!
  3. Bargaining: If I agree hot chocolate is a soup will you leave me alone?
  4. Depression: Now I'll never be able to eat salad the same way again...
  5. Acceptance: Fine. Guess I'll bring my spoons next time I go to the beach then.
